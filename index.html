Learning from the best. Come and join us!

Do you plan to go on a roadtrip? Maybe you are looking to start a nomadic lifestyle? Or maybe you simply need a license to get around town and run errands? EcoAutokoulu is here to save the day!

Why are we the best?

Professional teachers

Driving professional lessons can be expensive and stressful. Develop your driving skills, confidence, and flexibility with a professional driving instructor. You can also learn new driving skills or knowledge directly from the best driving instructors in Helsinki.

Training materials

Best for learners who want to learn how to drive, or for those who want to drive in their new country. The driving classes are conducted in English, but our teachers also speak Finnish.

Flexibility

You can schedule your service online, in person, or over the phone. We understand that tight schedules can compromise the nature of a busy schedule. That's why our teachers are available to work with you in the time you have.

Driving with us...

Your best learning experience is a mere hour and a half away, but instead of leaving the traditional classroom, we bring school to you. At EcoAutokoulu, it is still possible to learn by doing when you have lessons, but with a modern twist. The classes for your driving skills and the driving track for professional driving will help you achieve your goals, but the experience and thrill that comes with getting your license is incomparable. Classes of all levels from beginner to professional are on offer.
